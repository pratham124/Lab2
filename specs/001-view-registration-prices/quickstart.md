# Quickstart

## Goal
Verify the attendee pricing view displays active categories and handles empty/error states per UC-20.

## Steps

1. Open the Registration Prices page as a logged-out user.
2. Confirm active categories with prices are visible.
3. Confirm inactive categories are hidden.
4. Confirm categories without amounts show “Not available”.
5. Remove or deactivate all priced categories and confirm “pricing is not available”.
6. Simulate a retrieval failure and confirm a friendly error message with no technical details.
